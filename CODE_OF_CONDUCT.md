# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We want participation in this project to be a harassmentâ€‘free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and kind in language and behavior.
- Assume good intent; ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and helping users.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory remarks.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information (doxxing) without explicit permission.

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, and any official community channels).

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers by opening an issue requesting private contact, or by using the contact mechanism described in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans from project participation.
